# Consumable Details

Per-consumable notes that appear on the detail screen between the recipe and the properties. Each section is keyed to an item by an HTML comment containing the item code on the `###` heading.

Format:

    ### Berserkir Mead <!-- MeadBzerker -->
    Notes here. Supports **bold**, *italic*, bullet lists, and tables.

`##` category headers are for author organization — they are not rendered on the detail screen. Only `### Title <!-- Code -->` sections are extracted.

## Meads

### Berserkir Mead <!-- MeadBzerker -->

You need to buy a [Toadstool](1) from the Bog Witch (post-Moder) for 85 coins. 
 
**Reduces attack, block, and dodge stamina usage by 80%**, but the player takes **1.5x physical damage** from Blunt, Slash, and Pierce.

Pairs well with the [TrophyBonemass]* Bonemass power to negate the weakness.

Similar to tasty mead, this mead has **no cooldown** and can be constantly consumed.

Many players choose to bring this to boss and mini-boss battles to help improve their DPS output.






### Fire Resistance Barley Wine <!-- BarleyWine -->

This mead makes you **very resistant to Fire** damage and is generally useful in the Ashlands but also helps in other key situations:

* Fighting Black Forest mini-boss Brenna
* Fighting Cultists in Mountain Frost Caves
* Raiding Fuling villages to negate Shamans
* Engaging a Gjall in the Mistlands

**WARNING** You must consume the mead prior to taking the Fire damage.







### Anti-Sting Concoction <!-- MeadBugRepellent -->

You need to purchase [FragrantBundle](5) from the Bog Witch (post-Moder) for 140 coins.

Makes you **immune to Deathsquitos**, which still attack but you will take no damage.





### Brew of Animal Whispers <!-- MeadTamer -->

You need to buy [PungentPebbles](5) from the Bog Witch for 125 coins. 

This mead makes the **taming process 2x faster** for all animals in a 60m range. If a creature is in range of multiple players with the buff, the taming speed is multiplied by each player. For example 3 players = 6x faster, so you can tame in 5 mins instead of 30 mins.



### Frost Resistance Mead <!-- MeadFrostResist -->

This mead grants Frost Resistance for 600 seconds (10 minutes), which **prevents the Cold and the Freezing Effect**. It also reduces Frost damage.

**Highly useful in the Mountains** but also useful in other biomes to remove the Cold debuff at night.

Once you have Frost Resistance gear, you'll no longer need this mead as the effect will not stack.

**WARNING** You must consume the mead prior to taking the Frost damage.






### Lightfoot Mead <!-- MeadLightfoot -->

This mead **reduces jump stamina cost** by 30% and **increases jump height** by 20% for 10 minutes.

This mead is especially useful in the Mountains, Mistlands, and Ashlands.


### Lingering Healing Mead <!-- MeadHealthLingering -->

This mead provides **+25% HP regeneration** for 5 minutes. While active, it prevents the consumption of all types of healing meads.

Many players prefer the other healing meads as they provide nearly instant healing.



### Love Potion <!-- MeadTrollPheromones -->

These potions can be purchased 5 for **110 coins from the Bog Witch**.

Upon consumption, it will **increase spawn rate of Trolls** from 5% to 25%. If you are 2000+ meters from spawn, starred Trolls are also more likely to spawn. All Trolls will be **automatically alerted** to your presence.

1-★ Trolls spawn rate 9% → 87.5%
2-★★ Trolls spawn rate 1% → 12.5%

**Dear IronGate** - please make this potion work for Boars, Wolves, Seeker Soldiers, and Asksvins







### Mead of Troll Endurance <!-- MeadStrength -->

You need to buy [PowderedDragonEgg](5) from the **Bog Witch for 120 coins**.

**WARNING** the trollfish used for crafting must be of the exact same quality.

This mead will increase your carry weight by 250, effectively the same as the [BeltStrength]*.


### Poison Resistance Mead <!-- MeadPoisonResist -->

This mead makes you very resistant to Poison damage for 10 minutes, reducing the tick damage by 75%.

**WARNING** You must consume the mead prior to taking the Poison damage.

Highly useful in the Swamp and situational in other Biomes:
* Use near tar pits in the Plains
* Use in the Infested Citadel during the Queen fight


### Tonic of Ratatosk <!-- MeadHasty -->

You can buy the [CuredSquirrelHamstring](5) from the **Bog Witch for 80 coins**.

This mead increases player walking and **running speed by 15%**, and **swimming speed by 7.5%**.

Loved by speedrunners for the Haste effect, don't forget to collect plenty of blueberries.


### Tasty Mead <!-- MeadTasty -->

This mead gives **Health regen -50%** and **Stamina regen +100%** effectively trading slower health regen for faster stamina regen.

This mead has **no cooldown** so you can consume it constantly.

The stamina regeneration effect will be interrupted if the user performs any action that normally consumes stamina, such as attacking or sprinting. Tasty mead may be useful in boss fights due to its short cooldown period and dramatic restorative effects.

Drink plenty while farming, chopping wood, mining, and other low risk activities.



### Draught of Vananidir <!-- MeadSwimmer -->

You must purchase [FreshSeaweed](5) from the Bog Witch for 75 coins.

This mead **reduces Stamina consumption when swimming** by -50% for 5 minutes.

When combined with Eikthir power, you can swim very far distances.



### Lingering Stamina Mead <!-- MeadStaminaLingering -->

This mead provides **+25% Stam regeneration** for 5 minutes. While active, it prevents the consumption of all types of stamina meads.

Many players drink this mead during boss and mini-boss battles.

Combine this mead with [TrophyTheElder]* Elder boss power for quickly chopping forest / mining ore.










### Major Healing Mead <!-- MeadHealthMajor -->

This mead **restores 125 HP** over 10 seconds. A 2-min cooldown prevents you from drinking healing meads back-to-back.

Many players prefer this mead over the Lingering variant as it restores HP more quickly.





### Minor Healing Mead <!-- MeadHealthMinor -->

This mead **restores 50 HP** over 10 seconds. A 2-min cooldown prevents you from drinking healing meads back-to-back.

### Medium Healing Mead <!-- MeadHealthMedium -->

This mead **restores 75 HP** over 10 seconds. A 2-min cooldown prevents you from drinking healing meads back-to-back.

Many players prefer this mead over the Lingering variant as it more quickly restores your HP.

### Medium Stamina Mead <!-- MeadStaminaMedium -->

This mead **restores 160 Stam** over 2 seconds. A 2-min cooldown prevents you from drinking stamina meads back-to-back.

Learning to use this mead can save your life. For example, it can keep you from drowning.


### Minor Stamina Mead <!-- MeadStaminaMinor -->

This mead **restores 80 Stam** over 2 seconds. A 2-min cooldown prevents you from drinking stamina meads back-to-back.

### Minor Eitr Mead <!-- MeadEitrMinor -->

This mead provides **+125 Eitr** over 10s and has a 2 minute cooldown where you can't consume other eitr meads.

### Lingering Eitr Mead <!-- MeadEitrLingering -->

This mead provides **+25% Eitr regeneration** for 5 minutes. While active, it prevents the consumption of all types of eitr meads.

Many players drink this mead during boss and mini-boss battles.
