# Gear Details

Per-gear notes that appear on the detail screen between the recipe and the properties. Each section is keyed to an item by an HTML comment containing the item code on the `###` heading.

Format:

    ### Iron Helmet <!-- HelmetIron -->
    Notes here. Supports **bold**, *italic*, bullet lists, and tables.

`##` category headers are for author organization — they are not rendered on the detail screen. Only `### Title <!-- Code -->` sections are extracted.

### Deer Hide Cape <!-- CapeDeerHide -->

Skip this cape unless you have extra deer hide and bones. This cape isn't really worth the valuable inventory slot unless level 4.


### Cape of Odin <!-- CapeOdin -->

Cape of Odin is a DLC armor piece that was available for the early supporters of Valheim via a Steam code. Codes were sent out in an email from Iron Gate.

### Feather Cape <!-- CapeFeather -->

This cape **reduces fall damage** completely and **limits fall speed to 5 m/s**. It provides resistance to Frost, however it is **very weak against Fire**.

### Linen Cape <!-- CapeLinen -->

This cape is unique in that you can choose from 6 colors:
* Red
* Blue
* Green
* Yellow
* White
* Black

Unfortunately, it does not protect the player from Freezing.

### Lox Cape <!-- CapeLox -->

This cape provides Frost resistence so you don't feel cold or freezing.

This cape is considered cheaper than the Wolf Fur Cape because it uses less silver and doesn't require a somewhat rare Wolf trophy.

### Wolf Fur Cape <!-- CapeWolf -->

This cape provides Frost resistence so you don't feel cold or freezing.

### Troll Hide Cape <!-- CapeTrollHide -->

Another cape you can skip unless you want the Troll set sneak buff.


### Asksvin Cloak <!-- CapeAsksvin -->

This cape provides a **-15% dodge Stamina** usage and the Wind run effect. **Wind run increases running speed up to 25% and decreases running stamina usage by 100%** based on the wind direction and wind strength.


### Ashen Cape <!-- CapeAsh -->

The Ashen cape gives **Frost resistence**, **-10% to attack stamina usage** and **-20% to block stamina usage**.

Best of all, it starts at **12 armor** and is the only cape with such levels of armor.
