## Overview

Full rules [on speedrun.com](https://www.speedrun.com/valheim?h=any-glitchless-test-ashlands-new-game-random-seed-normal&rules=game).

This guide targets early stone portals and fast as possible Flametal. 
Avoid death and focus build skills for swords. We prioritize
better gear/food before killing later bosses.

<div style="font-size: 1.3rem">

* [__Phase 1__](#phase-1) kill [Eikthyr]*, build home, bronze age, find [Elder]*
* [__Phase 2__](#phase-2) kill [Elder]*, find [Moder]*, find [Bonemass]*, build longship
* Break <small>(real sleep)</small>
* [__Phase 3__](#phase-3) explore south, [portal_stone]*, kill [Moder]*
* [__Phase 4__](#phase-4) [Iron]*, [Black Metal]*, [Black Cores]*, [Eitr]*
* [__Phase 5__](#phase-5) [Flametal]* and [GemstoneRed]* [GemstoneBlue]* [GemstoneBlue]*
* Break <small>(if necessary)</small>
* [__Phase 6__](#phase-6) [Totem]*, [Sealbreaker fragment]*, [Bell fragment]*
* [__Phase 7__](#phase-7) kill [Yagluth]* [TrophySeekerQueen]* [Fader]* [Bonemass]*

</div>

## Phase 1

<div style="display:flex;justify-content:space-between">
  <div>

* Defeat [Eikthyr]* and hang trophy (15m)
* Gather [Flint]* [Leather]* and other resources as you go
*   Listen closely for [Queen bee]* and lookout for shipwrecks
* Explore southward for good black forest (15m)
* Start base near the following resources (30m)
  * Oaks
  * Copper nodes
  * Black forest coast
  * Multiple burial chambers `/printseeds` allowed
  * Near Spawn if possible for boss powers
* Kill [Bear Trophy]* and cave [Troll Trophy]* for armor (15m)
* Do burial chambers for [Surtling core]* and [Elder]* Vegvisir (15m)
* Use troll farming to gather resources as fast as possible (45m)
  * Try to multi-task, make coal while mining, etc.
  * Sleep often to speed up production
* Setup portals and start towards [Elder]* (5m)
  * If north, consider a reset
  * If south, bring [Copper](6) for forging iron nails
  * For very short sailing, raft is fine but prefer Karve

  </div>

## Phase 2

<div style="display:flex;justify-content:space-between">
  <div>

* Keep heading towards [Elder]* (30m)
  * If you pass a big mountian, consider leaving a portal
  * Kill [Elder]* and get the [Swamp Key]*
  * Craft [Finewood bow]* for next two biomes
* Find swamp, kill at least two [Abomination Trophy]* for the root vest (1h)
  * Gather [Ancient Bark]* and [Ooze]* along the way
  * Enter Crypt for [Iron]* needed for longship and stonecutters
  * Watch for Bonemass runes
  * Kill [Leech]* and get [Bloodbag]* for meads
  * Find [Turnip seeds]* for the [Cauldron]* upgrade
  * Look for double surtling spawners for coal farms
* Work on better food and meads (15m)
  * Use [Cultivator]* to plant a garden
  * Build [Fermenter]* and brew [Frost resistance mead]*
  * Sleep as often as possible
* Keep heading south looking for big mountains (45m)
  * Use [Frost resistance mead]* to scout mountains
  * Find [Onion seeds]* and [Obsidian]* in boxes
  * Check structures and peak into caves to find [Moder]*
* Leave portals behind near bosses and resources

  </div>

## Break

If you found [Bonemass]* and [Moder]* you can now take a break. It is also very
important to find the Traders. Haldor and Bogwitch are almost critical to a good
run. Keep scouting black forest and swamps for them.
